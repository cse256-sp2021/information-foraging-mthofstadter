Apply & Enroll

Accepting Admission & Enrolling Procedure

Once you have been accepted for admission you must complete the following steps to enroll your child in the Washington University Nursery School:

  1. Fill out the enrollment form. Note: this must be filled out every year that your child attends WUNS. ENROLLMENT FORM
  2. Fill out the Developmental History Form. This form only needs to be filled out once DEVELOPMENTAL HISTORY FORM
  3. Fill out the Child Medical Examination Report
  4. Fill out the Immunization Consent/History
  5. Pay a nonrefundable deposit of one month’s tuition. This will be applied to tuition for the following May.

Applying to WUNS Procedure

To apply to WUNS please complete the following steps:

  1. Fill out the application for admission. This form only needs to be filled out once. APPLICATION
    • Alternatively, you may print out and return the pdf version: APPLICATION PDF
  2. An application fee of $40 is required when applying to the nursery school. After this is received, your child is placed on an enrollment list. You will be notified of acceptance in the March preceding the fall enrollment date. Upon acceptance, a nonrefundable deposit of one month’s tuition is due. This will be applied to tuition for the following May.
