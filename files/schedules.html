Schedules

Full Day Schedule

A full day schedule is 8:50 a.m. - 3:15 p.m. Parents are welcome to enroll their children in a morning and afternoon session. Enrolling in two sessions is encouraged for parents with busy schedules and is available for children of all ages.


Teddy Bears (Ages 2½ - 3) 8:50 - 11:45 a.m.
Bear Cubs (Ages 3-4) 8:50 - 11:45 a.m.
Big Bears (Ages 4-5) 8:50 - 11:45 a.m.
Panda Bears (Ages 2½ - 3) 12:30 - 3:15 p.m.
Sun Bears (Ages 4-5) 12:30 - 3:15 p.m.

Extended Half Day - Bear Tracks

Children who attend Bear Tracks are ready for more than half-day classes but perhaps not full-day schedules. Bear Tracks is offered Tuesday through Friday, 9:45 a.m. – 12:30 p.m., for afternoon students and Monday through Friday, 11:45 a.m. – 2:30 p.m., for morning students. Parents may enroll their children for participation in Bear Tracks for any or all days it is offered. Check out bear tracks for more info.
